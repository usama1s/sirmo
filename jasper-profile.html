Jasper van der Kooi

Co-Founder, CEO & CTO

Jasper is a Dutch IT Expert and co-founder of Sirmo. He coded the complex back-end of both the Sirmo Portal and the new version of Giveawayhopper (Giveawayhopper V2).

Hi! I'm Jasper

Hi, my name is Jasper. I am an ICT professional who, at the age of 21, already has extensive experience in the field. Since the beginning of 2021, I have founded Sirmo, together with Jochum. Within this company we immediately started working on our first platform concept: Giveawayhopper. Besides working on this beautiful concept, I like to spend my time with my girlfriend and family. I also like to work on my own projects to see how I can develop myself further.

My Story

In 2013, me and my friends spent most of their time playing Minecraft, a game that basically has no limits regarding what you can make or play. To change the game more than usual, one must write plugins or mods, in the games programming language: Java. I started teaching myself this language so me and my friends could make bigger and better things.

When I met Jochum, we came up with a lot of great concepts, that I was not able to work on with just the Java knowledge that I possessed. Therefore I decided to keep learning more things so I could work on the things we wanted to create.

Since 2021, we have made our collaboration official by founding the company Sirmo V.O.F. We started developing our biggest idea, Giveawayhopper (V2). This is a new platform that allows other entrepreneurs, companies, influencers or individuals to organically grow their audiences using Giveaways. With this, and future projects, we hope to empower the online reach of our customers.
