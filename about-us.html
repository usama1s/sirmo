About us

Welcome to Sirmo! We develop growth marketing tools

We are a SaaS developer based in the Netherlands (Groningen) and we develop software applications that help companies worldwide to increase their online reach.

In recent years, social media use has received a huge boost, by both companies and consumers. On average, we spend 2.5 hours a day on social media, which of course has a huge impact on companies and the way of advertising. Increase your conversion and approach your target group with the push of a button. We are constantly improving our tools and developing new solutions to take care of your marketing worries. Since 2015, we have been working hard to give every company, from startup to corporation, the opportunity to positively connect with their target group.

Our mission

With our E-products we want to help entrepreneurs create a digital community of customers. We help you build a digital reach and increase your social media followers, newsletter readers, website visitors, etc. You can approach this network directly and free of charge in the future. We want to make you independent from today's / mainstream advertising models.
