Jochum Beerepoot

Co-Founder, CEO & CDO

Jochum is a Dutch product- & webdesigner, entrepreneur and co-founder of Sirmo. He founded a giveaway community called SGTTB in 2015 and a giveaway website called Giveawayhopper in 2017. Using the Giveawayhopper techniques, Jochum has grown SGTTB's follower group to over 100,000 followers within 1 year.

Hi! I'm Jochum

Hi, my name is Jochum. I am a passionate entrepreneur and product- & webdesigner based in Groningen, the Netherlands. I'm in the lead of Sirmo, together with my co-founder Jasper. In my free time, I enjoy hiking, mountain biking and discovering the best terrace spots in the area.

My Story

In 2015, I started a giveaway and trading community on the gaming platform Steam, called Sirs Good Time Trading Business (Abbr. SGTTB). In this group, I organized monthly videogame giveaways for friends. Friends of friends started participating the group and before I knew it, 1000 members joined. I decided to build a website to automate the processes. This website was Giveawayhopper (V1). Using the techniques of this website, the community grew to 100,000+ members.

In start 2019 the game giveaways stopped due to the increased videogame prices. In 2019 I teamed up with Jasper and in early 2021 we founded the company Sirmo V.O.F. We started the development of Giveawayhopper (V2), this new platform allows other entrepreneurs, companies, influencers or individuals to use the same techniques as I did back in 2019. With Giveawayhopper and our future growth solutions we want to give everyone the opportunity to grow.
